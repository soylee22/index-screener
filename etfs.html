How to read it. ★ stars = the Dip-Buy Meter from the 3-year high (a recent, actionable pullback measured in the fund's own volatility). Trend ▲ = above the 200-day average. AUM sorts biggest-first. Note: most market ETFs sit near all-time highs in calm markets, so few will show stars, the value is when a correction comes.
